Instructions

The rules are simple: take it in turns to attack. Each attack results in the lost of health points (Health Points). If you run out of Health Points, you die and you lose the game. If you rid your opponent of their Health Points first, they die and you win!

Every time you attack, your attack power is increased by its base rate. I.e. if your original attack power was 5, then it will increase by 5 points each time. Your opponents attack power does not increase.
