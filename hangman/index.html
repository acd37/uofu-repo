Instructions

If this is your first time here, this is a simple version of Hangman, but with a gin-infused (get it?) spin.

  1. Hit "Play" to generate a random word.
  2. Select letters by typing them into the box and then hit the "Submit" button.
  3. If you guessed a letter correctly, the letters will generate inside the word.
  4. Guess incorrectly, and you lose a life!
  5. Rinse and repeat until you're finished!
